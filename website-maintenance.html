FAQ's

What is website maintenance?

Website maintenance is more than just the process of keeping your website, it’s about ensuring your website performs well, loads quickly, ranks well and converts optimally. Some common web design tasks include Theme updates, plugin updates, WordPress / CMS updates, page speed optimisation, fixing broken links. With a well maintained website, that’s in great health, you stand the best chance of generating new business through your website.

Why is website maintenance important?

Website maintenance is important because it ensures that your website is not only secure but always functioning optimally. Neglecting your website could lead to not only performance issues but also security issues where your website not only becomes slow but also becomes vulnerable to hackers who could tamper with your website or use it to host spammy pages causing you further problems with your SEO, your web host and even cause your leads & enquiries to drop.

Website maintenance packages explained

Website maintenance package is the perfect way to keep your website well maintenance and always up to date. Consider it like an MOT of a car – Ensuring everything runs smoothly and addressing any little niggles before they potentially become bigger issues. As a website owner there is a lot to keep on top of: plugin up dates, theme updates, code updates, page speed updates, SEO updates and of course the general day to management of your website. My website maintenance packages will take care of these items for you and allow you to get on with the planning and growth of your business. There are a number of website maintenance packages available for you to choose from, see above for details and prices.

What does website maintenance cost?

Maintaining and further developing your website should not be a chore or expensive task. You have likely already invested hundreds of pounds into your website to get it where it is today. Therfore it makes sense to have a website maintenance budget to ensure you keep your website nice and healthy. Website maintenance costs start at £50 per month going up to £125 per month.
